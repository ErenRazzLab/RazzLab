Fee Policy

Razzlab charges modest fees to maintain the platform, support development and ensure fair draws. The fee structure is transparent and applied to sellers at the time of creating a razz.

Hosting Fee

A hosting fee of 5% of the total pot is applied to each razz. This fee is automatically deducted from the seller’s earnings after the razz completes. Fees may vary for promotional events but will always be clearly communicated.

Mini Razzes

Mini and micro razzes incur a reduced hosting fee of 2% to encourage more participation and fun. Mini winners who advance to a main razz do not pay additional fees.

Payment Processing

Any third‑party payment processing fees (e.g. Stripe) are passed through at cost. These fees will be itemized during checkout once payment integration is enabled.
