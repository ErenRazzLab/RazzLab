Data Deletion Request

If you wish to remove your Razzify account and delete all associated personal data, you can do so at any time. Follow the instructions below to submit a deletion request.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email Request: Send an email to support@razzify.com from the address associated with your Razzify account. In your message, include your display name and a clear statement that you would like your account and data deleted. We will verify the request and remove your data within 30 days.
  2. In‑App Deletion: If you are logged in, go to your profile page and click the Delete Account button (if available). This will initiate the deletion process automatically.

What Happens Next?

Once your request is verified, we will remove your personal details, hosted razzes, collection tags and participation records from our databases. Some aggregated statistics may remain for analytical purposes, but they will no longer be linked to your identity. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

If you have any questions about our data deletion process, please reach out to support@razzify.com.
